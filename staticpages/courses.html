Courses

We offer VLSI courses for B.E/M.tech/Diploma students with Electronics/Electrical streams. The courses are conducted at our centers in RNSIT and JSSATE Bangalore on weekends. Students can register and enroll for the courses.

VLSI Layout Design Course and Internship Using 45nm Technology

Introduction to MOSFET

CMOS Basics: MOSFET Construction and Operation, Regions of Operation (Cut-off, Linear, Saturation), Threshold Voltage
IC Design Flow: Specification to Gds
Fabrication: Wafer Fabrication, Oxidation, Mask Generation, Photolithography, Diffusion, Ion-Implantation, Etching, Chemical Mechanical Polishing

Tool Introduction:

Schematics and Layouts Entry: Library Creation, Schematics and Layout Design, Bind-Keys
Stick Diagram: Euler's path, Area and Routing Optimization Techniques

Custom Layouts:

Introduction to Basic Layout Concepts: Flat and Hierarchical Layouts
Quality Checks: LVS, DRC

Standard Cell Layouts:

Concept of High Performance and High Density Library
Track Based Library Creation Concepts
ASIC Library Design: NOT, NAND, NOR, AND, OR, XOR, TG, AOI, OAI, ADDER, LATCH, D-FLOP

Memory Layouts:

Introduction to Different Types of Memories
Sub-block Design and Layout: Bit-cell, R/W Circuit, Sense-amp, Row Decoder, Control Block, 1KB SRAM

Analog Layouts:

Concept of Sheet Resistance
Layouts of Resistor, Capacitor, Op-amp, Matching, Shielding

Quality Checks:

Design For Manufacturing Rules
DRC, LVS, EMIR, Antenna, Latch-up, Density, Proximity Effects

Advanced Topics:

Short Channel Effects
STI, Finfet, Double Patterning, EUV

Student Workshop and Faculty Development Program

We conduct on demand tools training and VLSI workshops for students and faculty. This program can be requested by the college staff only.
